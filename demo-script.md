# Demo Script

1. Open the site and read the hero text on **Home** to explain what Macro Notes is and how to use it.
2. Click **See latest note** to show a free preview flow.
3. On the note page, point out the section labels: **Key idea**, **What to watch**, **What could happen next**, and **What could go wrong**.
4. Scroll to the bottom and show the **Partner reading access** box.
5. Go back to **Home** and click **Read full report**.
6. On the full report page, highlight the **Members-only** banner and explain free preview vs full report access.
7. Scroll through **Full report** and **Watchlist table** to show deeper detail.
8. Open **Access** from the top navigation and read the plain membership instructions.
9. Open **Free preview notes** from the top navigation and show card-level **Read free preview** actions.
10. Open **Partner access** and click one of the **View demo report page** links.
11. Enter a bad URL to trigger **Page not found**, then click **Go to home page** to demonstrate recovery guidance.
12. Return to Home and recap the three terms: **Free preview**, **Full report**, and **Members-only**.
